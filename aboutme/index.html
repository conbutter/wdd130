About Me

Connor Butterfield

Good morning, afternoon, evening, or whatever time you're reading this! I'm Connor, and I'm happy you came to my nice little webpage. I'm from the humid, hot state of
Florida, pretty close to Orlando. I'm currently majoring in Web Design and Development, to learn even moreabout how user interface can impact experience! Design is very,
VERY interesting to me- trying to figure out how users will interact with the things we create is imperative to building and maintaining well-designed and easy-to-use websites
and systems.

I love creating things- pretty much everything, actually! Music, games, videos, graphics, even digital recreations of entertainment venues and theme parks- you name it,
I've likely dabbled a bit into it. I feel learning more about the web and how we interact with it is critical as our world heavily relies upon websites and the systems designed around them.
I'm also obsessed with games- board games, video games- but not mind games. Let's make that distinction pretty clear here.

I grew up in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints and served in the Utah Provo Mission. I would not be the person who I am today without my Savior and
Heavenly Father, and I feel my desire to create is a gift and an invitation from them to make things that others can resonate with. if there's anything all of my
work is centralized around, it's trying to help others feel joy, inspiration, or hope from the things I make- and hopefully, this semester, I can hone my skills
to get even better at that.

My favorite scripture of all time is Alma 26:27, which states "Now when our hearts were depressed, and we were about to aturn back, behold, the Lord comforted us,
and said: Go amongst thy brethren, the Lamanites, and bear with patience thine afflictions, and I will give unto you success." This scripture gives me hope that,
as long as I have the desire, He will give me the strength to keep moving forward.

Thank you for taking the time to read about me!

Until next time...
